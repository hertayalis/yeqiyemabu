All New Armor and Apparel Locations - Fallout 4: Nuka-World

In Fallout 4 Nuka-world, the player will find many marketing inspired gear, Nuka-cola power Armor, clothes and jumpsuits etc around the post-apocalyptic pack. You can dress up as you desire with new apparel and armors. You can try out many COOL outfits in here. Even Raider gangs there have their own outfits. This article shows Fallout 4 Nuka World all new Armors and Apparels location and where to find them.

All New Armor and Apparel Locations

Western Outfit

Location – In the Nuka-world, When you take on “High Noon at the Gulch” quest, you will be rewarded with this by Sheriff Hawk in the Dry Rock Gulch.

  • Bonus: +1 CHR.

Cappy Glasses

Location – In the Nuka-world, for completing “Cappy in a Haystack” quest, you will get it by Sierra Petrovita.

  • Bonus: Eyewear allows to see hidden cappy images in the Park.

Hubologist Outfit

Location – In the Hubologist’s camp, if you collect 5 space suits in the “Trip to the Stars” quest, you will be rewarded with it.

Nuka-Girl Rocket suit

Location – In the Galactic zone, You will find it on animatronic riding the rocket in the exit area of Nuka-Galaxy.

  • Bonus: It helps in breathing under water and +1 CHR.

Nuka-world Jumpsuit

Location – In the Nuka-world, Once you complete “Cappy in a Haystack” quest, you will be rewarded with it by Sierra Petrovita.

  • Bonus: +1 INT and +1 PER.

Nuka-world Geyser Shirt and Jeans

Location – It is found in the Nuka-world market at Chase’s store, to be purchased for 7 caps.

  • Bonus: +2 END.

Cowboy Hat

Location – In the Nuka-world, When you take the quest “High Noon at the Gulch”, you will be rewarded with this by Sheriff Hawk in the Dry Rock Gulch.

  • Bonus: +1 END.

Nuka-world jack and Jeans

Location – It is found in the Nuka-world market at the Katelyn Alden’s store for the Purchase of 7 caps

  • Bonus: +2 END.

Nuka-World Cappy, Bottle Shirt, and Jeans

Location – It is found in the Nuka-world market at the Katelyn Alden’s store for the Purchase of 7 caps

  • Bonus: +1 LCK.

Spacesuit Costume Helmet

Location – It is found in the Galactic Zone. You will find some suits on mannequins in the area.

Spacesuit Costume

Location – It is found in the Galactic Zone. You will locate some suits on Mannequins in the area.

Overboss Power Armor

Location – It is found in the Cola-cars Arena, You will get it When you take on a quest “Taken For A Ride” from Overboss Colter.

  • Bonus: It does the Energy damage of enemies around you. Torso has the bonus effect.

Nuka T-51F Power Armor

Location – It is found in the World of Refreshment, behind the locked door. Get to the Security room by a Cracked wall, there you will have to unlock the power Armor cage by hacking the terminal to find it.

  • Bonus: +3 Agility with all pieces equipped.

Oswald’s Hat

Location – It is found in the Kiddie Kingdom at the King cola’s Castle, once you complete “A Magical Kingdom” quest as a reward or found dropped sometimes.

  • Bonus: Once you wear it, it increases your speed movements up to 10%.

Magician’s Tuxedo

Location – It is found in the Kiddie Kingdom at King Cola’s Castle as a reward or dropped for completing “A Magical Kingdom” quest.

  • Bonus: It reduces the damage from any human up to 15%.
