Duchess Kate Wore an Ivory Blazer and Skirt to Platinum Jubilee Concert

June 15, 2024
blog

Duchess Kate chose an elegant ivory outfit for the special Platinum Jubilee concert at Buckingham Palace.

The Cambridge family attended the star-studded BBC Party at the Palace concert, where the Prince William, Duchess Kate, and their eldest children Prince George and Princess Charlotte watched the festivities along with many other members of the royal family.

For the event, the duchess wore a monochromatic look, pairing a belted ivory blazer with a matching skirt including a sheer top layer. She accessorized the outfit with a small cross necklace and a pair of teardrop-shaped diamond earrings by Robinson Pelham, while wearing her hair lightly curled and draped over one shoulder.

NIKLAS HALLE'N//Getty Images

Jacob King - PA Images//Getty Images

Prince William and the Cambridge kids all wore their own outfits in colors from the British flag, including a bright blue suit for the Duke of Cambridge, a darker navy suit for Prince George, and a ruffled red dress and cardigan for Princess Charlotte.

The family were all photographed sporting huge smiles and singing along they enjoyed the show, at times waving miniature British flags.

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

The duchess stepped out in a bolder look for an engagement in Cardiff, Wales earlier today, where the Cambridges viewed the rehearsals for the city's own celebration concert at Cardiff Castle. Kate wore a fiery-red coat dress from London brand Eponine, which evoked the red dragon on the Welsh national flag, as well as gold earrings from Welsh designer Spells of Love.

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

Kate's concert look follows her radiant appearance at the Trooping the Colour parade Thursday, where she paired an ivory Alexander McQueen coat dress with a navy blue hat with white trim by Philip Treacy. She finished the look with color-coordinated jewelry, including a pair of sapphire-and-diamond earrings previously worn by Princess Diana, along with a matching necklace and her iconic 12-carat oval Ceylon sapphire engagement ring.
