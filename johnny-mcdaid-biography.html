Who is Johnny McDaid?

Johnny McDaid is a Northern Irish singer, songwriter, and musician.

In addition, he is also a record producer. He is popular for his work with the Scottish and Northern Irish band Snow Patrol.

With the band, he is a songwriter, guitarist, vocalist, pianist, and music producer.

Johnny is also famous for his collaborations with such artists as Ingrid Michaelson, Harry Styles, and Rosi Golan.

His songs have been featured on several TV programs such as One Tree Hill, The Hills, Pushing Daisies, and Defying Gravity.

Johnny McDaid: Professional Life and Career

Johnny McDaid has been an active member of the music industry since the late-1990s.

He began his music career as a singer and songwriter with the four-piece alternative rock band, Vega4.

At that time, he was the band’s lead vocalist and songwriter.  He became famous for the band’s second album, You and Others.

The band’s first single “Life Is Beautiful” became a hit among the audience.  The song was also used for the theatrical trailer of My Sister’s Keeper.

In 2009, he signed with Snow Patrol‘s publishing company, Polar Patrol Publishing. After that, he joined the alternative rock band Snow Patrol.

After joining the band, Johnny went on to play guitar and piano and sing backing vocals with Snow Patrol.

Johnny McDaid has written and produced tracks for famous artists such as Ed Sheeran, Harry Styles, Carina Round, Ingrid Michaelson, Gary Go, and Example.

In addition, he has worked with Birdy, Kodaline, Rudimental, Biffy Clyro, and Rosi Golan.

Many of his songs have been used in various advertising campaigns including Land Rover, Jeep, and RTÉ.

In 2010, he produced the “Snow Patrol Remix” of U2’s “Unknown Caller“.

Johnny then co-wrote the song ‘Say Nothing’ for Example’s album The Evolution of Man in 2012.

In addition, his musical compositions have been featured in several TV films including Pushing Daisies, One Tree Hill, and Grey’s Anatomy.

In 2017, Johnny McDaid co-wrote Ed Sheeran’s ‘Shape Of You‘ with Ed Sheeran and Steve Mac.
