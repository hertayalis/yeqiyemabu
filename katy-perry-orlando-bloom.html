Katy Perry Spent Her Saturday With Orlando Bloom and His Son Flynn

May 27, 2024
blog

Rumors of a relationship between Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom have been brewing for a while now, and the duo just dropped another telling clue about it. Over the weekend, the two were spotted taking Bloom's five-year-old son Flynn to a children's party at Robert Downey Jr.'s house in Malibu. 

TMZ snapped photos of the couple leaving the party together in the same car, with Bloom, 39, in the front seat and Perry, 31, in the back with Flynn, the actor's son with model Miranda Kerr. This is reportedly just one of several hangouts for the couple this month, a source tells People, and they're "very happy." 

Word of  a possible relationship first sparked when photos surfaced of the two flirting at a Golden Globes after-party (pictured above) last month. At the time, however, the news was difficult to believe, since since Perry was spotted with her on-again-off-again flame John Mayer at a New Year's celebration, while Bloom was reportedly linked to Selena Gomez.
