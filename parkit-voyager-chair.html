Guide to the All-in-One Camping Chair

May 28, 2024
blog

As an avid outdoorswoman, I can safely say that hauling armfuls of equipment through dense brush is arguably the worst part about enjoying the fresh air. In the Eastern part of Michigan, it’s not uncommon to have to hike several miles to find the best beachside spot. This isn’t an easy feat while carrying several pounds of camping gear. That’s why I—like the hundreds of hikers before me—plan to splurge on the viral Parkit chair.

What is the Parkit Voyager chair?

via merchant

The Parkit Voyager cooler chair sports an attractive geometric weave, a sturdy aircraft-grade aluminum body and wooden armrests. The ergonomic design boasts a generous amount of recline, making it easy to kick back and relax. The height of the chair is carefully adjusted to be comfy for arthritic knees. On the side, an interchangeable cup holder offers a place for Stanley Quenchers to sit.

Underneath nests a sneaky detachable cooler that keeps drinks cold for up to eight hours. Fill it with sodas, Bud Light, or simply use it as a way to keep food cool while camping. Waterproof pockets built into the top offer a place to store camping gadgets while on the go. Sports fans, rejoice—this also allows plenty of room for tailgating must-haves like sunscreen and fan gear, too.

The back of the chair features two backpack straps with adjustable cords, which make it easy to carry the chair along to and from the tent, stadium bleachers or beachfront. A backpack beach chair with a cooler underneath is a camping accessory that avid hikers never knew they needed. Not only is it useful for freeing up hands, but it takes up virtually no room once folded. Slide it right into your car’s trunk or under a seat.

Why Shoppers Love the Parkit Chair

As a fan of portable pizza ovens and smokeless fire pits, I always perk up when talk turns to the best camping chairs—especially ones that offer extra bells and whistles, like heated camping chairs. Showing up to a pal’s bonfire with drinks in tow is just good party etiquette, and everyone will “ooh” and “ahh” over this futuristic two-in-one. Chairs with coolers aren’t something we see every day, especially ones that are easy to get in and out of. Don’t stand up for too long, or someone might sneak a seat.

I’m not the only one excited about this backpack cooler chair, either. Reviewer Ryan M. writes, “I purchased two slate Voyager Chairs. I brought them to a friend’s house for a campfire and immediately, there were two more orders. The idea of having a multipurpose chair that is easy to bring anywhere and have beverages is genius.

I have utilized the coolers a number of times on the golf course, as they fit perfectly in the back of the golf cart. The chairs get a lot of attention wherever we go, the beach, a town baseball game or outside playing yard games. I highly recommend these chairs, as they are exactly what is advertised.”
