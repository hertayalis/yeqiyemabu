Who Is Arica Angelo From Love Without Borders?

July 06, 2024
blog

Love Without Borders is a brand new reality TV dating show that will premiere on Bravo in just a few days. The show will follow five single Americans who put everything on hold and relocate in search of true love.

The five Love Without Borders singletons will embark on an unknown journey outside of the United States. When they arrive, they will be paired with their ideal mate, as chosen by relationship expert Arica Angelo.

However, there is a catch. None of the singletons will know anything about their companion before they arrive at their destination. Because the trip is so short notice, they will be unable to communicate with their companion or even view a photo of how they appear prior to their arrival at the location. Arica Angelo, according to her Instagram profile, is one of the top ten relationship counselors. Arica Angelo Intimacy Experience is the CEO and founder of her company. She will match singles with their ideal mate on Bravo’s Love Without Borders.

Arica Angelo of Love Without Borders began giving advice as a child

Arica Angelo is from Santa Monica, California, and she has over 6,000 Instagram followers. She also has around 120k subscribers on YouTube. She is currently on the French Riviera. Her work is described in her LinkedIn bio as follows:

“Through intimacy, we assist influencers in birthing and nurturing their legacy.”

Her parents are pastors, and she began giving advice as a child. According to her website, when her parents were not at home and people called for advice, she would assist them by giving them the best advice she could.

According to her website,

“They’d call my parents after I’d spoken with them and given them my best advice, saying, ‘Your daughter, despite her young age, just gave me the best advice!'” In my journey of self-healing and working with countless healers, as well as spending more hours than I care to admit studying self-development and healing…I began developing my own methods of sharing this freedom with my new customers.”

While the Love Without Borders host enjoys providing humorous and practical advice on her YouTube channel, coaching is an entirely different experience.

She also stated on her website,

“I usually focus on inner healing and profound personal development in the lives of my clients. My clients may come in with questions like, “How should I speak to women?” or “Help, my relationship is falling apart,” but my goal for them is to be empowered and restored to the people they were meant to be.”

Arica worked in business development at Zabinet from 2015 to 2016 before becoming a relationship coach. Her Instagram page features videos of her offering relationship advice on a variety of topics.
