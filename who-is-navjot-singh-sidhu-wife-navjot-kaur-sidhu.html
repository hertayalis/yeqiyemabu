Navjot Singh Sidhu Biography

Navjot Singh Sidhu was born on 20 October 1963 in Patiala, Punjab. He did his schooling at

Navjot Singh Sidhu completed education at HR College of Commerce and Economics in Mumbai.

He got associated as judge of comedy shows, and as a permanent guest in Comedy Nights with Kapil. As a politician he was criticized by the people, but later on same people became his fans. Specially because of his one-liners during comedy shows.

Navjot Singh Sidhu is a cricketer by profession. He has also worked as the Minister of Tourism, Cultural Affairs and Museums of the State of Punjab. Sidhu was elected to the Lok Sabha from Amritsar in 2004 on a BJP ticket.

Sidhu tendered his resignation from the Lok Sabha after facing manslaughter charges in 2006. In 2009 he defeated his Congress opponent Surinder Singla by a huge margin of 77,626 votes. Sidhu did not contest the 2014 Lok Sabha elections.

Modi government nominated Sidhu to the Rajya Sabha in April 2016.

Contesting from Amritsar East in 2017 Punjab Assembly elections, he won the election by a margin of 42,809 votes.

Sidhu’s net worth is estimated to be ₹50 crores (as of 2016). He gets paid around ₹1,10,000 (as in 2016) plus some other allowances as an MLA.

Sidhu belongs to a Jat Sikh Family. His father, Late Sardar Bhagwant Singh, was a cricketer. He was also the Attorney General of Punjab.

His mother’s name was Nirmal Sidhu. Sidhu had two sisters, Late Neelam Mahajan and Suman Toor.
